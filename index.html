Music without restrictions

SuperQ is a cross-platform song queuing and collaborative playlist application. No longer does song queuing and playback have to be limited by streaming service. Collaborate and combine your favorite songs from your favorite streaming services all in one app!

Cross-Platform

SuperQ connects to your streaming service(s) of choice*

Collaborate

Build cross-platform queues with collaborators and preserve as a playlist

Listen

Mixed playlists are automatically translated to your streaming service of choice for playback

*Currently supported streaming platforms: Spotify, Apple Music, Deezer. More to come!
